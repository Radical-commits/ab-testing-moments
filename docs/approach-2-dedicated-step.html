Approach 2: Dedicated Step — A/B as a First-Class Step

A new "Test Setup" step is added between Recipients and Message Composition. Users choose between "Send to Everyone" or "A/B Test," configure the test, then proceed to side-by-side message editors. A/B testing is visible and structured as part of the main campaign flow.

+ A/B testing is visible and structured + Side-by-side editing is clear + Config separate from content - Adds a step to every campaign - Non-test campaigns have an extra click
