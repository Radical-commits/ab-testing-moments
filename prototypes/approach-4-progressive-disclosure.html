Approach 4: Progressive Disclosure — "Add a Variant" Inline

After writing the first message (Variant A), a prominent "Add Variant B" card appears below the editor. Clicking it expands an inline Variant B editor and test configuration. This approach makes A/B testing discoverable without forcing a decision upfront. Users can write their message first and then decide to test.

+ Natural writing flow: compose first, test second + Highly discoverable without being forced + Can be removed just as easily + No upfront decisions needed - Message step becomes longer when A/B is enabled - Config and content mixed in one step
